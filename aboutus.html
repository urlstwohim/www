Who is Family IT?

“Family IT strives to offer the most personalized experience to our customers. We place customer’s needs at our highest priority and it is the customer that matters most to us.”

Family IT is a Technical Services Provider. We offer Consumers and Businesses a wide range of technical services at an affordable price. For the consumer we offer remote assistance with different computer issues not limited to virus removal, network related problems, printer issues and other technical issues. For the business customers we provide professional services not limited to business analysis, web development, project management, and technical procurement. We also provide a virtual training environment for both consumers and businesses that offer a wide range of online classes at a flexible schedule.
